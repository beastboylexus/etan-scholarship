Our Story

This scholarship was founded by Dr. Egbert Aung Kyaing Tan, a psychiatrist who emigrated from Myanmar to the United States.

Through years of hard work and the generosity of others, he built a career in medicine and spent over three decades serving communities across America.

This trust is his way of paying that forward — ensuring that the next generation of Burmese students has the same chance to succeed.

The Scholarship

Five scholarships are awarded each academic year to Burmese students enrolled in US colleges and universities.
